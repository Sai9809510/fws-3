About Our Club

The FSW Club (Free Software Wing) at Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology (GRIET) promotes the use of open-source software among students and faculty. It encourages learning and developing software that is free to use, modify, and distribute. The club organizes workshops, coding sessions, and other events to introduce and advance students' skills in free and open-source software tools, helping them become proficient in areas like Linux, Python, and various development frameworks​( GRIET )​( GRIET ).
